Xiaochuan (小川), means little river:

A geodynamic modeler that loves climbing to the mountain peaks and sailing in the vast oceans.

I spend my seconds with 贝果/一凡 (Ethan, born in 2022), playing racket sports (tennis, badminton & ping pong), swimming, reading and exploring how the world works.

To realize the universal truth of the mind and matter, I meditate 2 hours a day since 20240828 after my first 10-day meditation retreat at Dhamma Dhara, which has profundly transformed my life positively.

I try my best to contribute to a better world with a brighter future.
